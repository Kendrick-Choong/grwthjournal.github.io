Custom to the User

Your journal uses machine learning models to create a truly unique experience. Each time you write a journal entry, the model will analyze your writing, and create new prompts based on what is most relevant to you! The best part is, as the model learns what you like, the experience will only become better!

Although your custom machine learning model will be reading through journal entries, no human will ever lay eyes on what you write. Rest assured, both the writing and the model are yours and yours alone. In addition, our platform uses encryption to ensure your account is safe and secure.
